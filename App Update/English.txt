** IMPROVED SEARCH **
In this version of SongShift you should experience a much higher success rate when importing and exporting songs. Improvements have been made across the board to ensure a higher success rate while keeping quality high.

** Exporting **
When SongShift first launched the aim was the help bring Spotify playlists to Apple Music. Today our aim is to keep you listening to your favourite music on the service you want. SongShift now lets you export playlists to Spotify.

** Fixes **
We found a couple bugs which were really causing some headaches for users. We put our best bug smashers up to the challenge and sorted these issues out.
